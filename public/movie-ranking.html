The Best Movies According to Harrison

A list of my favourite movies of all-time.

Bladerunner 2049

I'm a sucker for a good vibe, rain, neon lights, and a guy trying to figure out who he really is.

Dune: Part One & Two

It's about staying calm in the chaos, and proving you can handle way more than you think (even if it's sandworms).

The Dark Knight

The perfect mix of chaos, intensity, and “I could watch this ten more times.

The Batman

Brooding, methodical, and all about focus, plus, it makes detective work look way cooler than it probably is.

Porco Rosso

A flying pig, seaplanes, and a weird amount of heart, it just makes me happy.

Fast & Furious 3

Drifting, neon lights, and one of the best soundtracks ever. What more do you need?

Oppenheimer

I admire its intensity, moral weight, and portrayal of how brilliance can both build and destroy.

Predator

The ultimate “survival of the fittest” movie, muscles, mayhem, and one-liners for days.
